2. Personal Data & Location

To function properly, Halfy requires access to your precise geographical location. This data is used solely in real-time to calculate the midpoint and routes.

No location data is stored on our servers. All processing is done either locally on your device or via anonymized requests to our third-party mapping services.

5. Limitations & Future Updates

Public Transport: This feature is currently disabled. Real-time multimodal routing services (like Google Transit) represent a significant technical cost. We plan to implement it in the future if there is strong demand from our users.

Reservations: Direct integration with reservation services (like TheFork) is currently under development and will arrive in a future update.
